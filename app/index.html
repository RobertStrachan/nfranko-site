Technically Scripted

A romantic comedy of errors

It was a day like any other. Until I found myself fired from my job. Then I found my boyfriend cheating on me with his best friend. Oh, did I mention that some jerk spilled coffee on me?.

It turns out that jerk was Ryan Vexler, the biggest movie star on the planet. Now my picture is all over the internet because some paparazzi thought me yelling at him about it would make a cute story.

And then he went and told everyone I was his girlfriend. Why? Apparently, he needs to clean up his public image. Now I'm stuck pretending to be his girl-next-door real life love interest so he can get some movie role and get an Oscar. I mean, he's paying me but the truth is, I'd do it for free. It turns out that he's not such a jerk after all and I'm starting to seriously fall in love...
